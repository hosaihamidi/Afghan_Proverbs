Proverb Detail

با یک گل بهار نمی‌شود

One flower does not bring spring.

This proverb means that a single good event or effort does not guarantee complete success or a lasting result. It emphasizes the importance of continued effort, collaboration, and time in order to create lasting change—especially relevant in the context of community building or personal growth.
