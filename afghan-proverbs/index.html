Afghan proverbs capture centuries of cultural wisdom. Often poetic and metaphorical, they reflect the values, humor, and resilience of the Afghan people. This site is dedicated to preserving and sharing that heritage.

Featured Proverbs

صبری کن، ترش شیرین می‌شود

With patience, the sour turns sweet.

هر که بامش بیش، برفش بیشتر

The taller the roof, the more snow it bears.
