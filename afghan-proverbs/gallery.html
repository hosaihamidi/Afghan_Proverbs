کله خام، شوربا خام

Raw head, raw soup.

Inexperience leads to poor results.

با یک گل بهار نمی‌شود

One flower does not bring spring.

One effort alone cannot create change.

هر که بامش بیش، برفش بیشتر

The taller the roof, the more snow it bears.

More wealth or status brings more responsibility.

صبری کن، ترش شیرین می‌شود

With patience, the sour turns sweet.

Patience turns difficulties into blessings.
